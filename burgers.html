Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • 1/2 pounds ground chuck
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Red onion

Steps

  1. Mix egg into ground meat with garlic and Worcestershire sauce
  2. Make hamburger patties from mix
  3. Cook patties until golden brown
  4. Toast buns
  5. Dice vegetables
  6. Asseble and serve!
