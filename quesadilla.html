Quesadillas

Description

We love everything about these quick and easy Cheese Quesadillas that are ready in minutes. This recipe makes the best quesadillas, hands down!

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Mexican mix shredded cheese
  • Butter
  • Choice of salsa

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan to medium heat
  2. Add butter to pan
  3. Place tortilla on pan once the butter has melted
  4. Add cheese to torilla
  5. Fold tortilla and cook both sides until toasted
  6. Serve and enjoy!
