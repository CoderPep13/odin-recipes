Tacos

Description

Have a go at making these scrumptous al pastor tacos. Topped off with fresh lettuce, onions, sharp cheddar cheese, and a spicy salsa verde. These succlent flavors will leave your mouth asking for more!

Ingredients

  • Marinated pork
  • Corn tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Premade salsa verde

Steps

  1. Cook pork meat until cripsy
  2. Dice up vegetables
  3. Heat up torillas to liking
  4. Assemble tacos and enjoy!
